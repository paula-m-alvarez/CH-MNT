Rishi Sunak Will Be Next UK Prime Minister

Rishi Sunak will become the UK’s third Prime Minister in seven weeks after winning the leadership contest triggered by the resignation of Liz Truss last week. Sunak, the former Chancellor, enters Downing Street after a year of political and economic turbulence for the UK, facing the need to stem a fiscal crisis, a cost-of-living crunch, a potential recession, the ongoing impact of the war in Ukraine as well as the fall-out from the ‘mini-budget’ and subsequent reversal of the implied policies. So far, markets have reacted quite positively to his appointment and Gilt yields as well as GBP have recovered some of the losses taken over the last few weeks. Over the coming weeks, it will be important for markets to see whether the presumed fiscally more conservative policies will be able to stabilize the market over the medium term as well.
